# 🇨🇦 Business Setup – Complete Intake Form

## 1. Personal Information

- Full legal name
- Date of birth
- SIN (for tax purposes)
- Residency status
- Contact email
- Phone number
- Home address

---

## 2. Business Overview

- Business structure:
  - Sole Proprietorship / Partnership / Corporation
- Business activity description
- Industry
- Number of owners
- Start date of business

---

## 3. Business Naming

- Preferred business name
- Alternate names (2–3 backups)
- Language of business name (EN / FR / both)

---

## 4. Business Location

- Primary business address
- Mailing address (if different)
- Province(s) of operation
- Will you operate internationally? (Y/N)

---

## 5. Ownership Details

- Owner(s) full legal names
- Ownership percentages
- Residency of each owner

---

## 6. Financial Information

- Estimated annual revenue
- Expected monthly revenue
- Revenue model:
  - Hourly / Fixed / Subscription / Other
- Planned expenses (rough categories)
- Expected profit (optional)

---

## 7. Tax Setup

- Do you expect to exceed $30,000 revenue in 12 months? (Y/N)
- Will you register for GST/HST now? (Y/N)
- Will you register for QST (Quebec)? (Y/N)
- Fiscal year-end preference

---

## 8. Banking

- Do you want to open a business bank account? (Y/N)
- Preferred bank (optional)
- Signing authority (who controls account)

---

## 9. Operations

- Business email
- Website/domain (if any)
- Payment methods:
  - E-transfer / Credit card / Stripe / Other
- Currency used (CAD/USD/etc.)

---

## 10. Hiring & Payroll

- Do you plan to hire employees? (Y/N)
- Expected timeline for hiring
- Contractor vs employee usage

---

## 11. Legal & Risk

- Do you want liability protection? (Y/N)
- Do you want business insurance? (Y/N)
- Type of work:
  - Client-facing / Product / Both

---

## 12. Permits & Compliance

- Will you operate:
  - Online only / Physical location / Both
- Description of activities (detailed)

---

## 13. Preferences

- Do you want help choosing business structure? (Y/N)
- Do you want automated tax registration? (Y/N)
- Do you want incorporation guidance later? (Y/N)
