# Intake Questions

These questions are asked once at the beginning to understand the user's business.

## Questions

1. What kind of business are you starting?
   - Options:
     - Bakery

2. Where will your business be located?
   - Options:
     - British Columbia

3. What is your business name? (optional)
   - Input: text

## Notes

- Keep this form simple and quick.
- Prefill this data into later steps.
- Do not ask unnecessary questions here.

## Expected Output (Example)

{
  "business_type": "bakery",
  "location": "BC",
  "business_name": "Vedant Bakery Inc."
}
