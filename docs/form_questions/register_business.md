# Register Business (Form Step)

This step helps the user prepare information needed to register their business in British Columbia.

## Questions

1. What is your business name?
   - Input: text
   - Prefill from intake if available

2. What type of business structure?
   - Options:
     - Sole Proprietorship
     - Corporation

3. What is your business address?
   - Input: text

## Notes

- This is one of the main interactive steps.
- Keep inputs simple and user-friendly.
- No validation needed beyond basic input.

## Expected Output (Example)

Business Name: Vedant Bakery Inc.  
Ownership Type: Sole Proprietorship  
Address: Vancouver, BC
